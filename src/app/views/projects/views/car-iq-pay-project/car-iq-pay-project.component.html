Car IQ Pay

Payment platform for fleets

Payment platform that enables vehicles to directly initiate, validate, and complete secure transactions without credit cards. By integrating with existing tech stacks and using telematics, it offers detailed insights to fleets, reducing fraud and simplifying expense management with a consolidated transaction ledger. Merchants benefit from better customer understanding and targeted, location-based marketing opportunities.
